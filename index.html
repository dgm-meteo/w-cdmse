Announcement

    The climate data management, data sharing and exchange workshop will be organized by the Moroccan Meteorological Service : "Direction de la Météorologie Nationale", Morocco, on Thirsday October 28th, 2021.
    The workshop will take place online via the Zoom platform and the link will be sent to the registered participants in due time.

Background

The main purposes of the workshop are to:

  • Provide guidance and training on the principles, tools and procedures from both WIGOS and WIS, especially focusing on the latest developments,
  • Discuss and agree on ways forward regarding improving data quality and data sharing.

The main topics to be addressed are:

  • Introduction to methods and tools for the generation of climate datasets
  • Concept of and introduction to computing World Weather Records,
  • Concept of and introduction to computing climatological standard normals for 1991-2020,
  • Concept of and introduction to computing National Climate Monitoring Products,
  • Quality control and Homogenization of climate data time-series, homogenization methods,
  • Use of WIS to share climate data and products,
  • The WIGOS Data Quality Monitoring System (WDQMS) functions and webtool, Metadata,
  • Management of WIGOS metadata with OSCAR/Surface and implementation of WIGOS Station Identifiers.
